Profile

Hello, I'm a designer and front-end developer with 3 years of agency experience developing and designing web solutions for clients like Toronto Ribfest, Baskin Robbins and Harlequin Publishing. My educational background includes both an Honours BA in Communications at Toronto’s York University and an Honours Diploma in Creative Advertising at Seneca College. I'm a geek at heart who loves exploring the world of science and technology to find inspiration, innovation and whacky scientific fun facts. When i'm not enjoying some Game of Thrones or some science fiction, I'm working meticulously to turn white space into purpose.
